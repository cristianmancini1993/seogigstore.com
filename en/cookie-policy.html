Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Cookie Policy explains which cookies are used on seogigstore.com and how you can manage your preferences, in compliance with the EU ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC) and the GDPR.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to improve your browsing experience, remember preferences, and gather statistical information.

2. Technical cookies (always active)

We use technical cookies essential for the operation of the website (consent management, security, session). These cookies do not require user consent under applicable regulations.

  • df_cookie_consent — stores your cookie consent (duration: 12 months)
  • df_countdown_end — promotional offer timer (duration: 24 hours)

3. Analytics and marketing cookies (consent required)

Only with your explicit consent do we use third-party cookies for analytics, marketing and remarketing through:

  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and create remarketing audiences. Meta privacy policy: facebook.com/policy.php
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous traffic analysis. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ads — for conversion tracking and remarketing

These cookies are installed only after explicit user consent via the cookie banner that appears on your first visit.

4. How to manage cookies

You can accept or reject cookies via the banner shown on your first visit. You can also change your preferences at any time through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5. Consequences of refusing

Refusing non-technical cookies does not affect site usability or your ability to complete an order. Only the analytics and marketing features will be disabled.

6. Your rights

For all rights regarding personal data collected through cookies, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

7. Contact

For questions about our Cookie Policy or to exercise your rights: info@seogigstore.com
