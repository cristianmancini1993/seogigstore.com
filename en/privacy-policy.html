Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TERRA-STITCH INTERNATIONAL S.L. (the Data Controller) collects, uses and protects the personal data of users of the website seogigstore.com, in compliance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and applicable national data protection laws.

1. Data Controller

TERRA-STITCH INTERNATIONAL S.L. — Calle Aragón 150 — 08011 Barcelona, Spain. Email: info@seogigstore.com

2. Data we collect

We collect the following personal data when you place an order: full name, phone number, delivery address. We also automatically collect technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and analytics purposes.

3. Purpose of processing

Your data is processed exclusively to:

  • process your order and arrange delivery
  • contact you to confirm the order
  • handle returns or customer service requests
  • fulfil tax and accounting obligations under Italian and EU law

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on the performance of a sales contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR) and on compliance with legal obligations (Art. 6.1.c GDPR).

5. Data retention

Order-related data is retained for 10 years in compliance with Italian tax obligations. Technical browsing data is retained for a maximum of 12 months.

6. Data recipients

Your data may be shared with:

  • partner couriers for shipping (e.g. BRT, GLS, DHL, DPD, Royal Mail)
  • tax and administrative authorities when required by law
  • IT providers managing the site infrastructure (under a specific Data Processing Agreement pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR)

7. Your rights (GDPR)

At any time you can exercise your rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection by writing to info@seogigstore.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (www.garanteprivacy.it) or your national supervisory authority.

8. Transfers outside the EU

Your data is stored on servers located in the European Union. Any transfers outside the EU only occur to countries with an adequacy decision or under Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

TERRA-STITCH INTERNATIONAL S.L. reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be published on this page with the date of the change.

10. Contact

For any question about our Privacy Policy or to exercise your GDPR rights, write to: info@seogigstore.com
